1. Toss 2 dice and observe their sum. What is the expected number of tosses until you observe a sum of 11?


|            | d1 = 1 | d1 = 2 | d1 = 3 | d1 = 4 | d1 = 5 | d1 = 6 |
| ---------- | ------ | ------ | ------ | ------ | ------ | ------ |
| **d2 = 1** | 2      | 3      | 4      | 5      | 6      | 7      |
| **d2 = 2** | 3      | 4      | 5      | 6      | 7      | 8      |
| **d2 = 3** | 4      | 5      | 6      | 7      | 8      | 9      |
| **d2 = 4** | 5      | 6      | 7      | 8      | 9      | 10     |
| **d2 = 5** | 6      | 7      | 8      | 9      | 10     | 11     |
| **d2 = 6** | 7      | 8      | 9      | 10     | 11     | 12     |

$P(X=11) = \frac{2}{36} = \frac{1}{18}$

Geometric distribution expected value of number of tosses until sum of 11 = $\frac{1}{P(X=11)} = 18$

$$
X \sim Geom\left(\frac{1}{18}\right) = 18
$$
