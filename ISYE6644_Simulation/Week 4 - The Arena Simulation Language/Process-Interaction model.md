
Idea: Basically just a flowchart

Entity moves through various processes

At any time, many entities may interact with one another as they compete for resources

Arena takes this process-interaction approach to define simulations

Entities flow through a network of "modules" (sometimes called blocks) that describe their behavior, which can be represented as a flowchart

Example: People showing up to the barber, waiting to get served (maybe after waiting in line)
- Create (generate customer arrivals)
- Process (use the barber)
- Dispose (leave)

More info can be found in [[Modeling Approaches]]
